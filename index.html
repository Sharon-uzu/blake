About Blake
Blake is a simple on-demand, last-mile bike delivery service with the ultimate goal of ensuring that people can have their little packages moved to them at the shortest and safest possible time.

We are focused on exploring the amazing power of technology to solve logistics problems and thereby providing employment for our hardworking Nigerian youths.

Blake is currently operating in Port Harcourt, Nigeria.
About Blake Food
Welcome to Blake Food, where culinary delights meet seamless delivery! Blake Food is a remarkable feature within the Blake app that brings a delectable experience to your fingertips. Designed to bridge the gap between food vendors and food enthusiasts, Blake Food offers a platform where vendors can showcase their diverse menus, and users can indulge in their favorite dishes with the assurance of swift and efficient delivery.

Our platform empowers food vendors by providing them with a space to upload and showcase their menus, reaching a broader audience of hungry customers. Whether you're craving the sizzling flavors of local delicacies or the international palette of global cuisines, Blake Food has it all.
